About Our Toolbox

A free, all-in-one toolbox for everyday tasks

Our Purpose

Toolbox is a free, all-in-one utility platform designed to help you with everyday tasks. All tools are client-side only, meaning your data never leaves your device. No installation required - just open and use!

Available Tools

QR Code Generator

Create scannable QR codes from text or URLs for easy sharing.

Password Generator

Generate secure passwords with customizable length and character sets.

Calendar

Interactive monthly calendar with navigation controls.

Calculator

Basic arithmetic calculator for quick calculations.

Unit Converter

Convert between different units of measurement (length, weight, temperature).

Text Case Converter

Convert text between different cases (upper, lower, title, sentence).

Timer/Stopwatch

Countdown timer and stopwatch for time tracking.

Color Picker

Select colors and get their hex/RGB values.

Base64 Encoder/Decoder

Encode text to Base64 or decode Base64 to text.

Word/Character Counter

Count words, characters, and sentences in your text.
